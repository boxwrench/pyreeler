# Vocabulary Map

Use this file as a map of creative territory, not a restrictive effects list.

## Visual systems

- geometry
- particles and swarms
- fields and flow
- noise and texture
- grids and lattices
- cellular and growth systems
- text and graphic overlays
- symmetry, mirrors, and kaleidoscopic structure
- masks, occlusion, and reveal systems

## Audio systems

- drones
- pulses
- arpeggiation
- noise beds
- clicks and glitches
- shimmer and high-frequency energy
- silence and negative space
- code-driven synchronization or intentional desynchronization

## Temporal systems

- recurrence
- entrancement
- delay
- acceleration
- deceleration
- suspension
- rupture
- return
- fade, bleed, cut, collapse

## Narrative functions

- establish
- foreshadow
- build
- suspend
- corrupt
- reveal
- overwhelm
- dissolve
- return
- resolve

## Material and surface

- CRT
- phosphor glow
- surveillance decay
- neon bloom
- clinical minimalism
- dust, grain, smear, damage
- glass, fog, signal haze, bleed

## Textual and symbolic strategies

- title cards
- terminal language
- captions
- fragments
- poetry
- warnings
- machine readouts
- symbolic recurrence

## Use rule

Choose from these families when they help the piece, and extend them when they do not.
The map exists to broaden options, not to replace artistic judgment.
